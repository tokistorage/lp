Browser Settings

Your browser also has built-in options to adjust font size and color scheme.

  • Zoom in/out: Ctrl (Mac: ⌘) + / - keys, or mouse wheel while holding Ctrl/⌘
  • Dark mode: Enable "Dark Mode" in your operating system settings
  • High contrast: Windows: Settings → Accessibility → Contrast themes
