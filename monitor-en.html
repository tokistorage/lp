Monitor Program
Experience it. Share your voice.

We're looking for monitors to experience TokiStorage's paid products through the full purchase flow and share their feedback. After your experience, we'll send you an Amazon gift card for the equivalent amount as a thank-you.

Why we don't give it away for free: Instead of handing out free samples, we ask monitors to go through the real purchase flow — payment, delivery, and usage. Only someone who has actually paid can tell us "who else would find this worth buying." That resolution is what we need to improve the product.
