Pearl Soap is named after Pearl, a beloved dog who was part of the family since 2007.

After Pearl passed in 2025, the feeling of "thank you for being here" was shaped into something tangible. Each time you hold this paw-shaped soap, you remember that someone precious was here —a small, everyday moment we want to deliver.

Pearl Soap is not for sale. It is given as a gift by Takuya Sato, founder of TokiStorage, to those with whom he shares a connection. Gift economy —a cycle of giving without expecting return.

Precautions

If irritation occurs or the product does not suit your skin, discontinue use immediately. Continued use may worsen symptoms. We recommend consulting a dermatologist.

(1) If redness, swelling, itching, or irritation appears during use
(2) If the above symptoms appear after skin is exposed to direct sunlight

  • If the product gets in your eyes, rinse immediately with water.
  • Avoid use around the eye area.
