Five Principles of 1,000-Year Design

Why TokiStorage chooses to design outside institutional frameworks

1
🪨

Material Permanence

Quartz glass (98% SiO₂) resists weathering and corrosion. Earth's most stable recording medium.

2
🔌

Zero Power

No servers, no cloud. Designed without predicting the technology landscape a millennium from now.

3
📱

QR Universality

Just hold up a smartphone. The simplest possible reading method was chosen by design.

4
🌐

Distributed Custody

Metadata published and distributed on GitHub. No single point of failure.

5
🏛

Extra-Institutional

Governments, companies, currencies — none guaranteed to exist in 1,000 years. We depend on none.
