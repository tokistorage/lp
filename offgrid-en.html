Common Concerns

"Don't I need an electrician's license?"

The scope requiring professional certification is limited. Portable power stations, solar panels, and satellite internet can all be set up without one — and that alone creates a fully functional environment. When licensed work is needed, we can connect you with the right professionals.

"Isn't the upfront cost too high?"

You can start small. Begin with a single solar panel and a portable power station, then expand as needed. By investing incrementally, you can grow your setup while keeping risk low.

"What if something breaks and I can't fix it?"

That's exactly why we insist on simplicity. Fewer parts, off-the-shelf components, designs you can maintain yourself. The reason we could build complete setups on Maui within three days is because we don't over-complicate things.

"Isn't this just a hobby — not really practical?"

On Maui, we actually lived in these setups and worked remotely. The Silkie chicken coop runs on automated solar power management. Our benchmark is whether you can live comfortably day to day.

Four Phases

Assessment — Site Survey & Consultation

Two free sessions. We review your site's location, climate, existing infrastructure, and goals. Together, we determine what's needed and what's not.

Design — System Planning (2 months)

We design the optimal combination of power, water, and communications. From equipment selection to layout planning and budgeting — keeping things simple, scalable, and free of over-investment.

Build — Construction Support (2 months)

Procurement assistance, installation guidance, and initial setup. The goal is for you to understand and be able to build it yourself.

Optimize — Fine-Tuning & Independence (2 months)

Adjustments based on real operating data. Balancing generation and consumption, adapting to seasonal changes, troubleshooting. We walk alongside you until you can maintain the system on your own.
