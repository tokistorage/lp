Sound Familiar?

"Don't I need an electrician's license?"

The scope requiring certification is limited. Portable power stations, solar panels, and satellite internet can all be set up without a license — and that alone creates a fully functional environment. When licensed work is needed, we help connect you with the right professionals.

"The upfront cost must be huge"

You can start small. Begin with one solar panel and a portable power station, then expand as needed. Incremental investment lets you grow your setup while minimizing risk.

"If something breaks, I can't fix it"

That's exactly why we insist on simple designs. Fewer parts, commodity components, maintainable by you. The reason we could build setups in under 3 days in Maui is because we keep things uncomplicated.

"Isn't this just a hobby project? Not really practical?"

In Maui, we lived and worked remotely on these setups. The silkie chicken coop runs on automated solar power management. Our benchmark is "can you live comfortably on this?" — and the answer is yes.

Four Phases

Assessment — Site Survey & Needs Analysis

Two free sessions. We assess your location, climate, existing infrastructure, and goals. Together we figure out what you need — and what you don't.

Design — System Architecture (2 months)

We design the optimal combination of power, water, and communication systems. Equipment selection, layout planning, and budgeting — all focused on simple, expandable design that avoids over-investment.

Build — Setup Support (2 months)

Equipment procurement support, installation guidance, initial setup. The goal is for you to understand and be able to assemble it yourself.

Optimize — Fine-Tuning & Independence (2 months)

Adjustments based on real operational data. Balancing generation and consumption, adapting to seasonal changes, troubleshooting. We walk alongside you until you can maintain everything on your own.

Why We Do This

TokiStorage's mission is "proof of existence" — carrying records a thousand years into the future. But if the places that hold those records aren't self-sustaining, the mission falls apart. Building infrastructure-independent sites is the foundation for connecting existence to the future.

Tied to Workaway Hosting

When starting a Workaway host operation in a rural area, one of the biggest hurdles is infrastructure. Without reliable internet, you can't communicate with guests. Without stable power, you can't offer comfortable accommodations. Off-grid consulting addresses the prerequisites that make Workaway hosting possible.
