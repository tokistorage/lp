Privacy Policy

Effective: February 1, 2026

TokiStorage ("the Company") handles personal information obtained in the course of providing TokiStorage services in accordance with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information, as described below.

1. Personal information collected

  • Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Content of inquiries and consultations
  • Photos, text, and voice data used for engraving
  • Payment-related information (credit card details are managed by the payment processor and are not retained by the Company)

2. Purpose of use

  • Provision of TokiStorage services and engraving production
  • Responding to inquiries and consultations
  • Service improvement and development of new services
  • Communicating important notices

3. Disclosure to third parties

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • With the consent of the customer
  • As required by law
  • To subcontractors necessary for service provision (payment processing, shipping, etc.)

4. Handling of engraved data

Data engraved on quartz glass is a physical record and is not stored on our servers. Data temporarily held for the purpose of engraving production will be deleted from our systems after production is complete.
