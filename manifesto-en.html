TOKISTORAGE MISSION

Existence, a Thousand Years Forward.

── 存在を、千年先へ。──

Ten Values

What we believe

I

Every person's existence deserves to endure a thousand years

Wealth and fame are irrelevant. The simple fact that someone lived is a record worthy of reaching the future. Opening the proof of existence to everyone is where we begin.

II

Guard with three layers; depend on none

Quartz glass, the National Diet Library, and the GitHub Arctic Code Vault. We entrust records to three layers built on different principles. The loss of any single point never means the loss of the record.

III

Permanence that requires maintenance is not permanence

We do not demand a thousand years of payments for a thousand years of preservation. Only a design that completes in a single act and endures without anyone's intervention deserves to be called truly permanent.

IV

Give first

Like Pearl Soap, we begin by offering without expecting anything in return. Giving breeds trust, and trust carries stories forward. Money is convenient, but it cannot reach the heart or memory.

V

A thousand-year perspective reveals what is essential

We are not swayed by quarterly results. Viewed from a thousand years hence, competition vanishes, trends fade, and only the essential remains. The choice of time horizon is itself the strongest differentiation.

VI

The memory of a place speaks louder than numbers

The fact that a Suwa Shrine has stood in the same spot for centuries is more eloquent than any hazard-map score. We honor the experiential knowledge of those who came before and inherit the wisdom that lives with the land.

VII

Some things are born only at crossroads

Semiconductors, consulting, cemetery research, repatriation of remains. Each discipline can be studied alone, yet what emerges from their intersection cannot be engineered. We take quiet pride in structural uniqueness.

VIII

Place new coordinates outside the frame

We do not compete with existing clouds. We do not fit inside existing funerary customs. We do not fight within existing capitalism. By standing beyond the frame, we see value no one else has noticed.

IX

Records grow in value with time

Data degrades and clouds disappear. Yet a single voice etched in quartz becomes history after a hundred years and a testament of civilization after a thousand. What we deliver is the only record that appreciates with time.

X

Inscribe the present for someone in the future

TokiStorage is not a gravestone company. It is the act of helping people alive today find meaning in their own existence. When someone a thousand years from now touches your voice, it becomes solidarity across time.
