Thank you so much for being a part of our family's life.

The time we've spent together, the words we've shared, the joy we've experienced — all of it is precious to us.

As a token of our gratitude for this connection, we would like to offer you this small gift of soap.

Pearl Soap

This soap is named after "Pearl," our beloved dog who was part of our family from 2007. After Pearl passed away in 2025, we wanted to give form to our feeling of "Thank you for being here."

Soap, once used, disappears.
But the time we shared together
stays with us forever.

This is not for sale.
It's a gift for those we're connected to.
