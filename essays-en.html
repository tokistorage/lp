CONCEPT

These essays take a single question as their starting point—“Why do we want to leave something behind?”—and explore the concept of proof of existence across diverse fields, from psychology and philosophy to economics, technology, and natural science.

They are not intended to criticize or antagonize any specific individual, group, or organization. All topics—including belief systems, political positions, and cultural practices—are discussed from a neutral standpoint. Any references or citations serve purely as material for inquiry, with no hostile or malicious intent.
