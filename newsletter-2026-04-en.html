The Technology and Philosophy Behind Voice Preservation

Standard format: Each issue of this newsletter features themed essays in the first half, followed by NDL-deposited customer TokiQR voices in the appendix. Every issue is deposited with Japan's National Diet Library, making all featured voices part of the nation's permanent archive.

1. Featured Essays

A curated selection of essays on TokiQR's design philosophy and business model.

  • 30 Seconds of Voice — How our proprietary audio codec fits up to 30 seconds of voice into a single QR code.
  • Technical Design — Why a server-free, fully self-contained design enables 100 years of permanence.
  • Deposition — Metal vapor deposition on quartz glass: the material science behind physical permanence.
  • 3-2-1 Backup Rule — GitHub, National Diet Library, Sado Island, Maui: the rationale for multi-site storage.
  • The Boundary of Made-to-Order — No refunds, made-to-order. Why we chose this business model.
