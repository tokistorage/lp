What is Workaway?

Workaway is an international platform connecting hosts with travelers (Workawayers). Travelers volunteer about 5 hours per day — helping with farming, cleaning, language exchange, and more — while hosts provide accommodation and meals.

  • Members from 170+ countries
  • No wages needed (volunteer basis)
  • Cultural exchange and language practice
  • Diverse host types: farms, guesthouses, private homes, and more

Four Phases

Assessment — Understanding Your Situation

Two free sessions. We listen carefully to your environment, goals, and concerns, and help you decide if Workaway is right for you. If it's not a fit, that's perfectly fine.

We uncover what makes your "ordinary life" extraordinary to international guests. Profile strategy, photo selection, and differentiation from other hosts.

Implement — Getting Started (2 months)

Workaway registration, profile creation (EN/JP), house rules, living space preparation. From going live to receiving your first applications.

Operate — Running Smoothly (2 months)

First guest onboarding support, message coaching, troubleshooting. We walk alongside you until you can run it on your own.
