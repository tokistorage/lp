What is TokiStorage?

TokiStorage records voice, photos, and text onto quartz glass and QR codes, preserving precious memories for long-term storage. With just a smartphone, anyone can create a "proof of existence."

Three Key Features

  • TokiQR — Convert your voice into a single QR code using your smartphone. It runs entirely in your browser, and no audio data is sent to a server.
  • Quartz Glass Recording — Data engraved onto quartz glass. A physical record with a theoretical durability of over 1,000 years.
  • UV Laminate QR — Photos and messages converted into QR codes and finished with weather-resistant laminate as keepsakes.
