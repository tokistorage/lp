Why this question, and why now

The one question AI cannot answer

AI generates optimal answers. But it cannot decide what to preserve.

Generative AI produces research, analysis, and strategy in seconds. Yet "What will you preserve for 100 years?" can only be answered from the context of your own life. This is not an optimization problem. It is a choice of meaning.

Wealth transfer is solved. Meaning transfer remains untouched.

Estate planning, business succession, asset management — the infrastructure for transferring what you own is mature. But the story of why you leave it — the narrative that gives inheritance its purpose — cannot be designed by institutions. You need a vehicle to carry your intent across a millennium.

Proof of existence is a gift.

What you leave behind becomes a gift to someone in the future. To your family. To your community. To generations not yet born. The knowledge that "someone was truly here" becomes an anchor for a stranger in a distant future — that is the essence of proof of existence.
