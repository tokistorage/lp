Service Overview

TokiStorage publishes newsletters as serial publications deposited with the National Diet Library (NDL) of Japan under the NDL Law (Articles 25 & 25-4). Receipt No. EA202602160140

This service opens our NDL publishing pipeline to businesses—wedding venues, event organizers, and corporations—enabling them to create their own branded newsletters deposited as permanent national records.

TokiStorage serves as the publisher of record. Your organization appears as the "content originator" in the colophon. Zero paperwork on your end. No technical setup required.

FAQ

What is the National Diet Library?
Japan's sole national library, mandated by law to collect and permanently preserve publications. Materials deposited are preserved indefinitely.
Can I choose the publication frequency?
Yes. Choose from quarterly (4x/year), biannual (2x/year), or annual (1x/year). Irregular schedules are also available.
Is an ISSN required?
No. ISSN is optional for NDL deposit. However, if you need an academic-grade identifier, we can handle the registration for ¥10,000.
Do I need any technical setup?
None at all. TokiStorage handles everything from repository creation to PDF generation to NDL submission.
Will the newsletter be publicly accessible?
Yes. Materials deposited at NDL are publicly accessible by default. The PDFs are also published on GitHub Pages.
