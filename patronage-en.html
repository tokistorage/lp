Why patronage

Not an investment. Not a donation. A third way to participate.

We have seen memories disappear.

Through SoulCarrier's work — finding burial destinations, surveying unclaimed graves — we have walked to and read over 10,000 headstones across Japan and abroad, one by one, with our own feet and eyes. Headstones inscribed only with "Unknown" broke our hearts. Not even a name remains. Lives weathered away into nothing. Memories vanish if left alone. TokiStorage was born to change that reality.

Proof of existence must not remain a privilege.

The technology to preserve memories in quartz glass for a thousand years is ready. But we cannot let it remain a service only for the wealthy. The unclaimed dead, communities that lost their records in disasters, people who pass without a voice — everyone deserves to leave proof they existed.

Supporting the mission becomes your own proof of existence.

Patronage is neither investment nor charity. Your name and intention are inscribed in the millennium archive as part of this mission. When the activity you supported endures for a thousand years, that itself becomes your proof of existence.

For corporate & institutional patrons

Practical considerations for your finance team

Processed like any other sponsorship or patronage

TokiStorage Patronage follows the same structure as cultural and social sponsorships — arts festival sponsorships, cultural heritage preservation, and nonprofit partnerships. Your finance team can process it within existing frameworks.

Typical accounting treatment

Often classifiable as sponsorship fees or marketing/CSR expenses. The patronage includes tangible deliverables: patron recognition and inscription in the millennium archive, supporting business expense treatment. Please confirm specifics with your tax advisor.

Qualified Invoice (インボイス) status

TokiStorage is currently a tax-exempt business and does not issue Qualified Invoices under Japan's invoice system. Under transitional measures, 80% of consumption tax is creditable through September 2026, and 50% through September 2029. Note that full expense deduction (損金算入) is available regardless of invoice status. We will consider registering as a Qualified Invoice Issuer in response to patron needs.

Documentation provided

We issue a sponsorship agreement, invoices, and receipts in standard business formats. Your accounting department can process them directly.
